// MISSION BRIEF

JUMP RUSH

A cyberpunk survival runner

⚡ OBJECTIVE

Survive as long as you can by jumping over obstacles racing toward you. Every obstacle cleared earns a point. Speed grows as you level up. You have 3 lives and if you lose all 3, the game ends💀💥.

🎮 CONTROLS

SPACE or ⬆️
Jump / Start the Game
P or ESC
Pause / Resume
Q
Quit / Forfeit the Game
TAP
Jump on Mobile / Tablet

🏆 LEVELS & BONUSES

  • LV 1 Base speed: warm up and get going 🦶
  • LV 2 Speed increases at 200 points 🏃🏃
  • LV 3 Double jump unlocked 🤸
  • LV 4 Faster obstacles at 600 points 🚀🚀
  • LV 5 MAX SPEED: survive if you can! ⚡⚡
